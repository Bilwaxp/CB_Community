PWOBLEM: Erreur "Missing required parameter: client_id"
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SOLISYON 1: Dezakte Google OAuth (TEMPORÈ - REKOMANDE)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Mwen deja dezakte Google OAuth nan kòd la
2. Bouton "Connexion avec Google" pa ap parèt ankò
3. Itilizatè yo ka konekte ak email/mot de passe sèlman
4. Redemare sèvè a pou chanjman yo pran efè

SOLISYON 2: Konfigire Google OAuth (Si ou vle aktive li)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Double-cliquez sou: CONFIGURER_GOOGLE.bat
2. Swiv enstruksyon yo pou kreye yon pwojè Google Cloud
3. Jwenn Client ID ak Client Secret
4. Antre yo nan script la

ETAP DETAYE POU KONFIGIRE GOOGLE:
──────────────────────────────────

ETAPE 1: Kreye yon pwojè Google Cloud
   1. Ale sou: https://console.cloud.google.com/
   2. Klike sou "Nouveau projet" (New Project)
   3. Bay yon non pwojè a (egzanp: CB_Community)
   4. Klike sou "Créer" (Create)

ETAPE 2: Aktive API a
   1. Nan meni a, ale nan "APIs et services" > "Bibliothèque"
   2. Chèche "Google+ API" oswa "Google Identity"
   3. Klike sou "Activer" (Enable)

ETAPE 3: Kreye identifikasyon OAuth
   1. Ale nan "APIs et services" > "Identifiants"
   2. Klike sou "Créer des identifiants" > "ID client OAuth"
   3. Tip aplikasyon: Application Web
   4. Non: CB_Community
   5. URIs de redirection autorisées:
      - http://localhost:3000/api/auth/callback/google
      - http://VOTRE_IP:3000/api/auth/callback/google
   6. Klike sou "Créer" (Create)
   7. KOPYE Client ID ak Client Secret

ETAPE 4: Konfigire nan .env
   1. Double-cliquez sou: CONFIGURER_GOOGLE.bat
   2. Antre Client ID ak Client Secret
   3. Script la ap mete ajou .env otomatikman

ETAPE 5: Reaktive nan kòd la
   1. Ouvri src\lib\auth.ts
   2. Dekomante (decommenter) liy GoogleProvider
   3. Redemare sèvè a

POU DEZAKTE GOOGLE (Si ou pa vle li):
───────────────────────────────────────
1. Double-cliquez sou: DESACTIVER_GOOGLE.bat
2. Script la ap dezakte Google OAuth nan kòd la
3. Redemare sèvè a

POU REZIME:
───────────
✅ Google OAuth deja dezakte nan kòd la
✅ Bouton Google pa ap parèt nan paj login
✅ Itilizatè yo ka konekte ak email/mot de passe
✅ Redemare sèvè a pou chanjman yo pran efè

Si ou vle aktive Google OAuth:
   → Double-cliquez sou: CONFIGURER_GOOGLE.bat
   → Swiv enstruksyon yo
