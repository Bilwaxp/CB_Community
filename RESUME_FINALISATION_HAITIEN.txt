POU KONFIGIRE GOOGLE OAUTH:
───────────────────────────
1. Double-cliquez sou: FINALISER_GOOGLE.bat
2. Swiv enstruksyon yo
3. Jwenn Client ID ak Client Secret nan Google Cloud Console
4. Antre yo nan script la
5. Redemare serveur la

ETAP DETAYE POU GOOGLE:
───────────────────────
1. Ale sou: https://console.cloud.google.com/
2. Kreye yon pwojè nouvo
3. Aktive "Google Identity API"
4. Kreye "OAuth 2.0 Client ID"
5. Konfigure URIs de redirection:
   - http://localhost:3000/api/auth/callback/google
   - http://VOTRE_IP:3000/api/auth/callback/google
6. Kopye Client ID ak Client Secret
7. Antre yo nan FINALISER_GOOGLE.bat
